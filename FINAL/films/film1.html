Catch Me If You Can

Based on the unbelievable true story of Frank Abagnale Jr., a master of deception who posed as a pilot, doctor, and lawyer—all before turning 21. This stylish cat-and-mouse thriller directed by Steven Spielberg pairs Leonardo DiCaprio and Tom Hanks in a dazzling game of wit and chase.
