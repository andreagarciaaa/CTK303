Superbad

Two awkward high school friends set out to enjoy one last wild night before graduation, hoping to score alcohol and impress girls. What follows is a chaotic, hilarious, and surprisingly heartfelt journey through teenage desperation and friendship. A modern classic of coming-of-age comedy.
