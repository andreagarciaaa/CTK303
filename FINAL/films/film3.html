Little Women

Writer-director Greta Gerwig’s fresh take on the classic Louisa May Alcott novel follows the March sisters—Jo, Meg, Amy, and Beth—as they come of age in post-Civil War America. A timeless story of ambition, love, and independence, brought to life with heart and brilliance.
